MorbidMeter & MorbidMeter Lite v2.6 Release Notes
Changes since v2.5
* Calendar timescales renamed (e.g. 'ONE YEAR' instead of 'YEAR')
* Reverse time now available in MorbidMeter Lite
* Days-Hours-Mins timescale added to both versions
* Days-Hours-Mins-Secs timescale added to full version
* Android 10 no longer supported
